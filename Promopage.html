Survivor is a reality television show that places a group of strangers in an isolated location, where they must provide food, fire, and shelter for themselves. The contestants compete in challenges for rewards and immunity from elimination. The contestants are progressively eliminated from the game as they are voted out by their fellow contestants, until only one remains and is given the title of "Sole Survivor" and is awarded the grand prize of US$1,000,000.

It involves strategic gameplay, beautiful locations, challenges and physical and mental fatigue. Watch contestants compete in this gruelling show, make alliances, blindside people....Wednesdays on CBS

Motto: Outwit, Outplay, Outlast
Host: Jeff Probst
